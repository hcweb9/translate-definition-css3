Computer

Definitions

  • Computer: A programmable electronic device that processes information, stores data, and communicates.

  • Compatible Computers: A computer that can use the same software and hardware as another computer, often referring to machines that share a common operating system or processor architecture.

  • Personal Computer(PC): A computer designed for individual use, typically smaller and more affordable than larger systems, and used for tasks like word processing, internet browsing, and gaming.

  • Portable Computer: A computer that can be easily carried around, such as a laptop or tablet, often equipped with a battery for independent operation.
